About

We like to joke that Fox and Chance's love for baseball is hereditary. Not only are mom and dad big baseball fans but their grandfather was a former minor league player. Baseball is life. Baseball has been life since they were five years old in tee-ball running the wrong way down the baseline and dancing in the field. Fast forward four years and baseball still fills the household every given second of any given day in the Davis home. Chance has become an avid pitcher and center fielder, while Fox has excelled at pitching and shortstop. Their love for baseball doesn't just stop on the field. They came up with a tee shirt business idea one day with their dad. With their fathers support and help, they got to work right away. That is how Ace and Dinger was born, with the goal of redefining casual baseball gear and making it available for all. Ace and Dinger makes custom and personalized clothing for the baseball lover in your life.
